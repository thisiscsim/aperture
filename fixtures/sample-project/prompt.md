# Japan at Christmas

Short-form vertical video (TikTok / Reels) about my trip to Japan over Christmas.

- Vibe: warm, cinematic, a little playful. Cozy winter energy.
- Length: ~20-30s.
- Hook: open on the most striking shot (snow + lights) in the first 2 seconds.
- Beats: arrival, city lights, food, a temple in the snow, a quiet closing shot.
- Captions: on, karaoke style, synced to any spoken audio.
- Music: upbeat lo-fi if provided.

Drop clips into `assets/` and run `/create-social-video japan-christmas`.
